COMMON QUERIES
Frequently Asked Questions

What types of granite do you offer?

We offer a wide range of granite including Black Galaxy, Absolute Black, Tan Brown, Kashmir White, Steel Grey, Vizag Blue, and many more with custom finishes.

Do you provide cut-to-size stone services?

Yes, We Stone specializes in cut-to-size orders based on your project requirements—be it for tiles, slabs, or countertops.

What is the difference between granite and quartz?

Granite is a natural stone known for its strength and natural beauty, while quartz is an engineered stone known for its consistency, colors, and low maintenance needs.

Do you handle delivery and installation?

Yes, We Stone offers end-to-end service including delivery and installation of all stone materials for residential and commercial projects.
